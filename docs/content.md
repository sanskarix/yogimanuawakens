# Medium Biography

Years ago I discovered yoga while searching for relief from chronic physical pain.

What began as a simple stretching practice gradually became a journey into meditation, mantra, devotion and conscious living.

Everything I teach today comes directly from personal experience. My hope is simply to share practices that have brought greater peace, clarity and healing into my own life.

---

# Long Biography

Before discovering yoga, I spent years working physically demanding jobs and living with persistent pain in my back, shoulders and knees.

A friend suggested trying yoga.

That simple recommendation quietly changed the direction of my life.

Through consistent practice I experienced healing that extended far beyond the physical body. Meditation, mantra and devotion gradually became natural parts of everyday life, opening new ways of seeing myself and the world around me.

My path has been deeply shaped by the teachings and grace of Neem Karori Baba, whose example of unconditional love, humility and service continues to inspire my own practice.

Today I share yoga classes, kirtans, conversations and reflections with the hope that others may discover their own path toward peace–not by escaping life, but by becoming more fully present within it.
